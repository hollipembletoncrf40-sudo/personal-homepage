// MY WORLD

This is the best of times.
This is the worst of times.
I choose to be prepared.

I'm a 00s kid who grew up with the internet and came of age in the AI era. We inherited tools that previous generations could only dream of — and with them, a world noisier than any that came before.

I read existentialism and history not as escapism, but as orientation. Human nature doesn't change. Power shifts, technology evolves, empires rise and collapse — but the patterns underneath stay the same. Those who read history aren't surprised by the present.

Business building fascinates me in the deepest way. Not as a vehicle for wealth, but as the most direct act of creation available to an ordinary person. To build something from nothing — a product, a community, a system — is to leave a real mark on the world. The universe is vast and meant to be experienced. I intend to experience all of it.

Compound interest is my core operating principle. Not just in finance — but in skills, trust, knowledge, relationships. Every habit, every page, every shipped project is a deposit. The math is patient. The results are not obvious until they suddenly are.

Opportunities in this era are not scarce. They are invisible to those who wait, and obvious to those who have been quietly preparing. I am a believer in the long game — in the compounding power of consistent, unglamorous daily work.

The future belongs to those who build it. I'm building mine — one day, one decision, one system at a time.

// 我的世界观

这是最好的时代，
也是最坏的时代。
我选择默默准备。

我是 00 后，生于互联网，长于 AI 时代。我们继承了前人梦寐以求的工具——但这也是人类历史上噪音最响的时代。

我读存在主义，读历史，读人性——不是逃避，而是找坐标。人性不变，技术更迭，帝国兴衰，但底层的规律始终如一。读懂历史的人，不会被当下吓到。

商业构建让我着迷，不是因为钱，而是因为它是普通人能做到的最接近创造的事。从无到有——产品、社区、系统——是在宇宙留下印记最直接的方式。大千世界本来就是来体验的，我打算把它体验个彻底。

复利是我最深的信仰。不只是利息，是技能、信任、认知和关系的叠加。每一个习惯、每一页书、每一个发布的项目，都是一笔存款。数学很有耐心，结果不显山露水——直到某天突然显现。

这个时代的机会从不稀缺。它对等待的人不可见，对默默准备的人却无处不在。我相信孙学、信仰长期主义——相信每天枯燥而坚定的积累，终将产生不可思议的力量。

未来由自己创造。我正在构建我的——一天、一个决定、一套系统。
